Hurstwood Lockdown TempO 20202

Due to the current lockdown we have organised another online TempO competition. This time in a technical moorland terrain with six stations of high difficulty.


Competition Format

  • TempO format
  • 6 stations
  • 5 problems at each station
  • 6 flags A-F with zero answers
  • 1 minute penalty for each incorrect answer. The difficulty of each station is high, read the map.

Map scale: 1:7500, 5m contours

Each station is an image from Google street view with six flags imposed on.

The map is a real orienteering map with control descriptions.

You may complete the competition at any time during the week. Your time will be recorded as the time taken from opening the form until the form is submitted and therefore will include the time taken to input minimal registration information.

Each page of the form will have one problem on it. Select the correct answer for each problem and then submit the form. It will help to open the image of the flags in a new window to view it at full size. A link is provided under each image to view it at full resolution.

Competition Ends: Saturday 21st April 23:59

Results will be published in full soon after.
